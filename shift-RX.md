# The Shift RX challenge  
staying in/with nature regularly for health improvement.

## daily
- 30 min (5 times/week)
- nearby nature (garden)

## weekly
- 2 hours
- woods, water ways, nature parks

## monthly
- 1 weekend
- restful natural areas

## yearly
- several days
- restful natural areas:
    - national parks
    - lakes
    - rivers
